ABOUT

      Luthando Mcuntula he was born 23 december 1992,In Eastern cape in the village called Ngxabaxha location. He was raise by her grandmother and she try by all means that he went to school,and she make him to became a good man. HE study his primary and junior at Buhlebelizwe Junior Secondary school.He was active and hard worker in his community, where the patience of helping started from. He Came to town in 2006. HE continue to help to other NPO AS volunteer, AND then he started his own foundation in 15 December 2018 in kanini Khayelitsha,as he have patience of helping people living with disability and old people. as he want to make difference to community.

vision

      The vision of Mcuntula foundation is to provide food for people who live with disability and old people. He want see everyhousehold have something to eat, He try to make them feel as normal people and having confident to themselves. He strive to end huger and poverty to disability and old people,STOP abuse against them to the community because the get rape and emotionally abuse. HE ensure that they must feel as the part of the community,them must treated as the same.
